Welcome to the Number Guessing Game!!!

Instructions:

  • There are 40 circular boxes.
  • A random number between 1 and 40 is generated.
  • You have 6 attempts to guess the correct number.
  • If your guess is too low, the circle turns yellow, and if it's too high, it turns red.
  • If you guess correctly, the circle turns green, and the game ends.
  • After 6 attempts, the game ends if you haven't guessed correctly.

Click the button below to start the game.
